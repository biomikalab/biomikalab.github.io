# Join as Member
- Currently, joining our division is subject to the Generasi Biologi Indonesia Foundation regulations and approval. Please contact the Generasi Biologi Indonesia Foundation if you are interested to be part of us.

# Research Volunteering/Thesis Students/Student Internship/Research Students 
- We <b>welcome</b> students for doing Bioinformatics research in our division! Students that are interested in research volunteering, doing research for final year thesis, doing internship, or participating in research are encouraged to reach out to the PI directly.
